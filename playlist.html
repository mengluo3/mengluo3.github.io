playlist

I like that music seems to deepen the world's colors and moods.

I have a tendency to overplay songs I like. After a certain point I have to take a break because the notes crumble into plain noise. As a result, my playlists are not named by genre of artist, but rather by date. Scroll through my Spotify profile and you'll find a timescape, each playlist a distinct period of time, with a different texture, in the context of a different set of memories.
