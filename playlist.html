I have a tendency to overplay songs I enjoy. After a certain point I have to take a break because the notes crumble into plain noise. As a result, my playlists tend not to be titled by genre or artist, but rather by date. In that way, a Spotify account can kind of act as an accumulation of life geological layers, except with music in place of sediment.
