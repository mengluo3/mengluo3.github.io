I like to create, and I like to share those creations. This site was born when I couldn't decide whether to post the 'Light and Water' video clips to Medium or Twitter. I realized my online presence was scattered across the internet, without a central core, so instead of choosing, I decided to just develop one giant collection of all my creations, adding a more personal touch with animations and formatting. My hope is for it grow into a life archive of sorts, documenting the evolution of my tastes and creations.

More on myself: I am an 18 year old living in Maryland, USA. Currently in the midst of a gap year, I graduated from the STEM magnet program at Montgomery Blair High School, class of 2018. My general interests lie at the intersection of humanities, arts, and tech. For more detail and resume information, contact me!

With that being said, welcome to this space I've carved out on the web :))
