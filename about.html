I like to create, and I like to share those creations. This site was born when I couldn't decide whether to post the 'Light and Water' video clips to Medium or Twitter. I realized my online presence was scattered across the internet, without a central core, so instead of choosing, I decided to just develop one giant collection of all my creations, adding a more personal touch with animations and formatting. My hope is for it grow into a life archive of sorts, documenting the evolution of my tastes and creations. With that being said, welcome to this space I've carved out on the web :))
