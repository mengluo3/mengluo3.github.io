The kids of 星星家园.

*Page under construction. I will update soon with some essays regarding my time at the center, the design of the volunteer program, and perhaps the state of special needs in China.*

February 18th to April 15th, 2019, I spent two months of my gap year volunteering at a special needs center. There I met some people that I hold dear to my heart.

Through these portraits that I discovered the magic ways that art can bring people together. Not only did the kids and the teachers of the center open up to me, requesting more sketches, people on the streets would often stop, watch, and start a conversation as I drew. Drawing — it's quite a good way to get to know people :)
